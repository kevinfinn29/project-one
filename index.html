The Seven Summits are the highest mountains of each of the seven continents. Summiting all of them is regarded as a mountaineering challenge, first postulated as such in the 1980s by Richard Bass. The mountaineering challenge to climb the Seven Summits is traditionally based on either the Bass or the Messner list. We consider the Messner list to be the most challenging and encompasses the greatest peaks the world has to offer. This is the challenge we set our guides and hopefully, YOU!
